THE BARBER SHOP

A barber (from the latin barber, "beard") is a whose occupation is mainly to cut, dress, groom, style and shave men's and boy's hair. A barber's place of work is known as a "barber shop" or a "barber's"

In the modern times, the term "barber" is used both as professional title and to refer to hairdressers who specialize in men's hair. Historically, all hairdressers were considered barbers. In the 20th century, the profession of cosmetology branched of the barbering, and today hairdresser maybe licensed as either barbers or cosmetologists. Barbers different with respect to where they work, which services they are licensed to provide, and what name they use to refer to themselves. Part of the terminology difference depends on the regulations in a given location.
